My name is Dustin Watkins and I am a Full-Stack Web Developer. I am eagerly developing software for online marketplace merchants, with long-term plans to develop applications that assist in the diagnosis and treatment of mental illnesses and disabilities. I have a passion for back-end development, specializing in node.js, frameworks (Handlebars and React), and relational-database design and management (MySQL). I have developed my skills by self-teaching, attending local community-classes, and capping it off by attending The University of Kansas Coding Bootcamp, where we learned the full-stack, courtesy of Trilogy Education’s curriculum. I also have a bachelor’s degree in Marketing. My true passion shines when things get challenging. Nothing gets me more excited than hearing, “That can’t be done.” I will stop at nothing to prove “It CAN be done.” There is an exciting journey ahead for me, and I can only hope that our paths might cross.
